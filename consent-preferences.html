What is Consent Preferences?

Consent Preferences allows you to control how Uventex Sports Hub collects and uses your personal data across our platform. You can customize your privacy settings for event registration, athlete profiles, membership data, and communication preferences to ensure your information is handled according to your comfort level.

By managing your consent preferences, you maintain full control over your digital footprint while still enjoying personalized sports event experiences and relevant updates about competitions, rankings, and membership opportunities.
